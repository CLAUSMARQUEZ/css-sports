FC Barcelona, also known simply as Barcelona and familiarly as Barça, is based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Paris Saint-Germain

PSG were founded in 1970, following the merger of Paris FC and Stade Saint-Germain. The club was split into two in 1972, with PSG being relegated to Division 3, before returning to Division 1 in 1974. The Parisians won their first major honour, the French Cup, in 1982 and their first Division 1 title in 1986.
